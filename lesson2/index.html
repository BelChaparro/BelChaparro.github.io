Name:

Clever-Weather

Domain:

www.clever-weather.com

Purpose:

The purpose of the Clever-Weather site is to provide the weather summary and a five day forecast for the towns of Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven. No matter the device that the visitor is using, this site intends to be a community space that provides information about activities, relevant news, and other creative content in each town according to the weather conditions.
