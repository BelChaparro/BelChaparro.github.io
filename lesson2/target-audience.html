Target Audience:

The target audience for the Clever-Weather site are adults, most likely between the ages of 20-65, that want to know the weather conditions and/or are looking for information about local weather news and/or available activities to plan their day/week.
They are middle class and will will probably visit the site from a mobile device.

Scenarios:

#1:

A group of young adults wants to go out to a cultural activity and need to the weather and temperature to know what to wear or if they'll need a jacket for later.

#2:

An adult couple wants to have dinner under the stars tomorrow night and needs to know the weather conditions to see if they'll be able to carry on the preparations.

#3:

A backpacker is planning a week trip to one of the local towns and wants to know what activities will be available while she's there and the weekly forecast to know what to pack.

#4:

A photographer wants to shoot animals near the river and needs a cloudy day to enhance the desired effects, he also intends to avoid crowds on site. He checks the page to see the gaps in between the local events and when he'll get the perfect conditions.

#5:

A woman checks the site every day before going to work to decide what to wear and whether she'll drive or take the bus. She doesn't like to drive when it rains.

#6:

An amateur weather journalist checks the site to take record of the local weather news that will make his collection interesting.
